# Sigidrigi — Song Timing Guide (for editors)

Timing is what makes the lyrics scroll in time with the song in the Sing screen.
It takes 1–2 taps per song. This guide is for anyone adding timing.

## How the timing model works

Every song has a **singing window**: the moment singing starts, to the moment the
song ends. The lyric sheet scrolls steadily between those two points. That's it.

- **Start** = your tap (saved as the song's intro).
- **End** = your tap, or the track's own end (one button).

## Quick Sync — the normal way (1–2 taps)

1. Open the song → tap the **⏱ timer icon** (Sing screen header, or the ⏱ in Admin → Songs).
2. Choose **Quick sync**. The backing track starts playing.
3. **TAP the big button exactly when the FIRST WORD is sung** — not when the music
   starts. This is the single most important tap. If you miss it, hit **Undo** or
   **Restart** and go again.
4. Then either:
   - Tap **"Ends with the track"** (YouTube songs — instant, no need to listen through), or
   - Listen to the end and tap **TAP — SONG ENDS** at the last sung word.
5. **Save**, then **Test in Sing Mode**. Watch the first verse — if the green line
   matches the singing, you're done.

Songs with no backing track: play the song on YouTube/Spotify yourself, and tap
along the same way (start + end) — the app uses a stopwatch instead.

## If the timing feels off

- **Lyrics arrive early or late right from the start** → the START tap was off.
  Re-run Quick Sync and tap tighter on the first word. Half a second matters.
- **Starts fine but drifts mid-song** → normal for Quick Sync: it scrolls at a
  steady average pace, so an instrumental break or solo makes the words run ahead
  during the break and catch up after. Two options:
  - Live fix: the **Pace slider** in the Sing screen (0.5×–1.5×) nudges the speed.
  - Proper fix: use **Line-by-line karaoke sync** (below) for that song.
- **Band plays slower/faster than the record** → that's what the Pace slider is
  for during a session. Don't re-time the song for this.

## Line-by-line sync — for perfect karaoke

For songs that deserve perfect timing (popular ones, songs with long breaks):

1. ⏱ → **Line-by-line karaoke sync**.
2. The track plays; **tap once as each line starts being sung**. The screen shows
   the current line big and green — tap on its first word.
3. **Undo** steps back one line; **Pause** if you need a breather.
4. Tap all lines → **Save**.

This makes the karaoke view follow every line exactly, breaks included.

## Rules of thumb

- Tap on the **first sung word**, always. Anticipate it — you know the song.
- Quick Sync first. Line-by-line only where it matters.
- Always **Test in Sing Mode** before moving to the next song.
- Set the **Key** and **genre** in Admin → edit while you're on the song — one pass per song.
